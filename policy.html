POLICY

Innovage Investment Ltd, with address Salama Park, Lusaka, Zambia ("Care365 Doctor" or "we") is a company established in Zambia, and as such, we comply with applicable data protection legislation in Zambia.

We believe that personal privacy is important and we take your privacy very seriously.

In our privacy policy, we explain what types of personal data we may process and for what purpose. We also explain our processing of personal data and which choices you have about it.

SCOPE OF POLICY

Please note that the privacy policy refers to the processing of personal data for which Care365 Doctor is the personal data controller. The privacy policy applies when you share your data directly or indirectly with Care365 Doctor when you visit our website or use any of our services.

The privacy policy does not cover the processing of personal data that Care365 Doctor performs as a personal data processor as a result of your use of our services through your insurer. For more information on how our services can be used to process personal data when you use our services through your insurer, please refer to your insurer's privacy policy. You can also contact us at privacy@care365.clinic or via our contact form if you have questions regarding our processing of personal data on behalf of another personal data controller.

PERSONAL DATA PROCESSED

Personal data refers to data that can be attributed to you. We may process the following types of personal data that can be attributed to you:
  1. Contact information ("Account information") such as name, e-mail address, and telephone number.
  2. Health information ("Health data") that you provide to us. As healthcare providers, we will never share Health Data with a non-medical third party (medical third parties can be, for example, labs for tests, NPÖ, physical health centers, and insurance companies).
  3. User behavior including digital behavior (for example, behavior within the application) ("Digital Behavior").
  4. Technical data (eg device ID, IP address) ("Technical Data").

HEALTH DATA PROCESSED

When you use our service, including your appointment with a doctor, you may share personal information about your past and present physical and mental health. This information may include, but is not limited to, information that you suffer from any disease, your medical history, or your physiological or biomedical condition (“Health Data”). Health data that is specifically attributable to you may also be provided by the doctor you come into contact with when using our service. The fact that the doctor adds information is part of the doctor's obligation towards you as a patient.

As a healthcare provider, Care365 Doctor operations are subject to the Patient Data Act (2008:355) and are therefore obliged to process (including archiving) Health data that arises in connection with your use of our service.

We do not share Health Data with third parties outside of our service, except in cases where such disclosure is required by law (e.g. the Patient Data Act). To be able to offer you our services, we also use a few external service providers who process personal data (including health data) on our behalf.

If you have been referred to Care365 Doctor by your insurer, Care365 Doctor can, for the settlement of a specific insurance case, release information about your state of health, including a copy of your medical records, to your insurer. Care365 Doctor makes this disclosure in the capacity of personal data assistant vis-à-vis your insurer and this Privacy Policy is therefore not applicable to that treatment. For more information about this treatment, we refer you instead to your insurer's data policy.

PURPOSE OF THE PROCESSING AND LEGAL GROUNDS

The purpose of the processing and the legal basis for the processing of the respective categories of personal data are as follows:
  1. We process Account Information to be able to offer Care365 Doctor services (i.e. to be able to fulfill the agreement between us), including enabling us to contact you to e.g. monitor your user experience. We may also use Account Information for our legitimate interest in conducting marketing activities, e.g. for sending newsletters.
  2. We process Health Data based on your consent to provide Care365 Doctor services and to fulfill all applicable legal obligations. It includes that our doctors keep medical records.
  3. We process Digital Behavior based on our legitimate interest in improving the mobile application, understanding and analyzing user behavior, and improving the user experience.
  4. We process Technical Data based on our legitimate interest in understanding users and for marketing purposes, for example in so-called "remarketing".

DATA STORAGES

Personal data is stored as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purpose (including the purpose of fulfilling the legal obligations incumbent on Care365 Doctor). Personal data that is stored based on your consent is deleted if consent is withdrawn. Note that the withdrawal of consent does not affect Care365 Doctor's right and obligation to process personal data by applicable laws, e.g. the Patient Data Act. Redundant data is anonymized and saved after the purpose of the data has been fulfilled.

TRACKING

To enable improvements to our services and your online experience, our software automatically collects information from your computer (or mobile device), and your browser, including your public IP address and domain name, cookie information, hardware characteristics (e.g. device ID ), websites you have visited, videos and images you have seen on our website, URL from websites referring traffic and after navigation to our websites, date and time of your visit and your geographical location. The data is compiled to help us gain a better understanding of behavioral patterns and trends within our services. To make this possible, we use web logs or applications that recognize your computer and collect information about your computer's online activity. We also work with certain third parties to collect and analyze parts of this information.

We use both session and permanent cookies on our websites. A cookie is a small amount of data, often including an anonymous unique identifier, that is sent to your browser from a website. If your browser is set to accept cookies, the cookie is saved on your computer's hard drive. Your browser will accept access to cookies that the website sends to your computer.

Our cookies provide information that facilitates your use of our website. Our cookies collect information about your use of our website during your current session and over time, your computer's operating system and browser type, your geographic location, the website you visited before our website, and links you used to leave our website. On some web pages, cookies enable our websites to remember information about our visitors' language choices and country of origin. You can change our collection of information by changing the cookie settings in your browser.

INFORMATION SHARING WITH THIRD PARTIES AND TRANSFER TO THIRD COUNTRIES

We may share personal data with our data assistants for the reasons stated above. Some of the personal data assistants may be established outside the EEA. We ensure that the personal data assistants we work with live up to adequate security standards. To ensure an adequate security standard, for example, the EU's standard data transfer clauses are applied or (if the transfer is to the USA) by ensuring that the personal data processor is connected to the Privacy Shield.

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to withdraw your consent regarding the processing of personal data based on your consent. Because we are a healthcare provider, we are required by law to retain most of your Health Data. At your request, all Health Data that we are not legally obligated to retain will be deleted.

You have the right to receive information about whether we process your data, for what purpose it is processed, whether such personal data is transferred to third countries, and, if applicable, who received your data.

You have the right to have incorrect information about yourself corrected. In certain circumstances, including when it has been established that we are processing personal data without a legal basis or the processing is no longer necessary to fulfill the purpose, you have the right to have the data deleted (the right to be forgotten). If the accuracy of the personal data or the legal basis for processing is in question, you can request that the processing of the data be restricted. You have the right to object to treatment that takes place based on Care365 Doctor's legitimate interests. In the event of such an objection, Care365 Doctor is obliged to demonstrate a legal basis for the continued processing of the personal data. You can indicate at any time that your data should not be used for marketing purposes.

You have the right to receive your data, to the extent provided by you, in an electronic format that is generally used. You have the right to transfer such data to another personal data controller.

We are committed to conducting our business by the principles set out above to ensure that the confidentiality of your data is protected and maintained.


CONTACT US

To update, correct, or delete the information we have about you, use rights that accrue to you according to the above, or get in touch with our personal data representative, please contact us at privacy@care365.clinic.
